Ekaterina Oleinikova

Ekaterina Oleinikova is an internationally acclaimed oriental dance artist and choreographer with more than 20 years of experience in belly dance.

• Two-time IDO World Champion.
• Three-time Dance World Cup Champion.
• Five-time Ukrainian Champion.

A sought-after workshop lecturer and competition judge in over 30 countries, she has built a reputation for precision, artistry, and uncompromising standards.

Her students consistently become winners and finalists of major international competitions — a reflection of her discipline and methodology.

For Ekaterina, belly dance is not defined by titles alone. It is a path to strength, refined posture, confidence, and feminine harmony.
